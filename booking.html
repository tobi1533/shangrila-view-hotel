Our Story

Shangri-La View Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury and excellence in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. Founded with a vision to redefine hospitality, our hotel has been dedicated to providing guests with an unforgettable experience that seamlessly blends elegance, comfort, and world-class service. Located in the prestigious Rumuosi Victory Estate along the iconic Airport Road, we have established ourselves as the premier destination for both business and leisure travelers.

Over the years, Shangri-La View Hotel has earned a stellar reputation for its commitment to excellence. From our meticulously designed rooms to our state-of-the-art facilities, every aspect of our hotel has been carefully crafted to ensure that your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to anticipate your needs and exceed your expectations.

Our Mission, Vision & Values
